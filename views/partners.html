The MSRC would like to thank its partners for their continued support: State of Maryland, Economic Development Administration (EDA), and the Mid-Shore Counties: Caroline, Dorchester, and Talbot.

The support of these partners enables the MSRC to contribute to other local initiatives: Eastern Shore Entreprenurship Center (ESEC), Eastern Shore Regional GIS Cooperative (ESRGC), Maryland Broadband Cooperative (MDBC), Maryland Upper Shore Transit (MUST).

Additional resources include Maryland Farming, Business, Economic and Community Outreach Network at Salisbury University and Maryland Department of Labor & Licencing.
