The Mid-Shore Regional Council (MSRC) operates as a cooperative regional planning and Development agency within Caroline, Dorchester, and Talbot Counties to foster physical, economic, and social development. The Council initiates and coordinates plans and projects for the development of human and economic resources.

The MSRC is a federally designated Economic Development District (an economically distressed area) that enables federal funding to the region through the U.S. Department of Commerce's Economic Development Administration.
